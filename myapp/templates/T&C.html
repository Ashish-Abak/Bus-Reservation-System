Terms of Service

Role of AshBus
AshBus operates solely as a technology platform connecting travelers with bus operators. We do not own or operate buses nor act as agents for bus operators. The booking voucher issued by AshBus is based on information provided by the bus operator regarding seat availability.

Limitation of Liability of AshBus
AshBus is not responsible for bus operations, including timely departure, conduct of bus operator employees, condition of the bus, or any loss or damage of customer baggage.

Responsibilities of Users
Users must contact the bus operator for boarding point information, carry a copy of the ticket and valid ID, and arrive at the boarding place 30 minutes before departure. All tickets issued are non-transferable.

Cancellation of Ticket
Ticket cancellations can be done via AshBus's website, mobile app, or customer care. Cancellation charges apply as mentioned on the ticket.

Rescheduling of Ticket
Ticket rescheduling can be done through AshBus's website, mobile app, or customer support. Charges apply as mentioned on the ticket, and rescheduling is subject to availability and policy.
